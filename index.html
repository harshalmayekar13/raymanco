Product Description ⌄
RayMan Men's Sunscreen SPF 50+ PA++++ is a broad-spectrum daily sunscreen engineered for male skin. Clinically tested using in-vivo methodology. Lightweight, oil-free formula absorbs instantly with zero white cast. Enriched with Niacinamide 5% for brightening and Hyaluronic Acid for 8-hour hydration.
How to Use ⌄
Apply a generous amount (¼ teaspoon) to face and exposed areas 20 minutes before sun exposure. Reapply every 2–3 hours during prolonged outdoor activity. Use as the last step in your AM routine, after moisturiser.
Ingredients ⌄
Active: Octinoxate 7.5%, Oxybenzone 3%, Avobenzone 3%, Zinc Oxide 5%. Inactive: Aqua, Niacinamide (5%), Hyaluronic Acid, Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Extract, Dimethicone, Glycerin, Tocopheryl Acetate, Sodium Hyaluronate, Allantoin, Phenoxyethanol.
